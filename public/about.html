MISSION STATEMENT

The problem we tackle: Misleading information from Youtube, Reddit, and influencers has led women to take advice from non-professionals, or to take diet pills or follow an unhealthy diet to achieve the idealized female appearance.

Our Solution: FemFit seeks to focus on women and focus on female related health without the self-doubt, mistreatment, and the risks of underqualified instructors.

Our emphasis is on educating users about certifications, empowering them to make informed choices. By highlighting female-specific concerns, such as pregnancy/post-partum exercise and dietary differences, we want to give a voice to the under-represented female demographic. Additionally, our service gives female professionals a place to shine. Most importantly, FemFit is here to neatly organize all your fitness sessions and health needs in one platform.
