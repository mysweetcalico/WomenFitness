Frequently Asked Questions


What are the benefits of using FemFit as a user?

As a user of FemFit, you will benefit from:

  • Easy-to-use search engine to find your certified female professional who can accomodate to your fitness and nutritional needs
  • A database of exercise statistics to visualize your fitness journey
  • A single portal to see all your booked schedules and progress
  • Amazing support backed by fellow fitness gals, as well as the dev team at FemFit.

What are the benefits of using FemFit as a trainer?

As a trainer of FemFit, you will benefit from:

  • An online platform that specifically features certified female trainers like you for the female audience.
  • An simple booking and communication process with clients
  • A single portal to see all your trainees, schedules, and ??
  • Amazing support backed by fellow fitness gals, as well as the dev team at FemFit.

Does FemFit cost anything to join or use?

FemFit is a free service for you to use. You only pay for the fitness sessions based on the trainer you book. There are no hidden costs and all beneficial assets like the Calendar and Progress graphs are free for you to use.

What types of trainers are available on FemFit?

At FemFit, we aim to help clients find a personal female trainer who empathizes with their clients' circumstances. That's why we highlight trainers who have experience with pregnancy, puberty, special needs, older age, and more in order for you to be able to do yoga, pilates, running, and other styles of fitness in the way you need.
